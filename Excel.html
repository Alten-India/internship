Welcome to the Alten Screening Quiz

This quiz is designed to help you assess and strengthen your foundational knowledge of commonly used corporate tools. The goal is to understand your:

  • Basics of Microsoft Excel and PowerPoint
  • Fundamentals of Cloud Storage & Data Backup

To proceed to the next stage, you must achieve a minimum score of 6 or above. Your responses will be recorded and evaluated.

Upon successful completion of all quiz sections, you will be guided through the next steps, which include creating your personal ID and submitting essential documents such as your CV and resume. You will also be required to answer a few basic screening questions. All submissions and responses will be reviewed by our team to determine subsequent actions.


Before beginning the quiz, we highly recommend watching the accompanying video tutorials to refresh your understanding.

Good luck!
